What are the IdTronix used for?

SUMMATRON-API allows a company to identify its users if they send a text generated by the server and that they must send to the company through the TRON network.


For the verification information to reach the company it is necessary that the shipment is made through a specific token, and for this we have created IdTronix.


IdTronix is ​​an instrumental token, not a cryptocurrency for the payment of the service. That's why 10,000 million IdTronix have been created at a price of 1,000 IdTronix for 1 TRX.


The objective is for SUMMATRON-API to replace the use of SMS or Google Authenticator, on the one hand for the cost and on the other, for the ease of use by not having to type the received codes, but read a QR.


A company that implements SUMMATRON-API, must buy some IdTronix, can distribute them among its users and when they use them they will return to their TRON account.
The goal is for the process to be quick, easy and cheap. Right now the price of an IdTronix would be € 0.00002 (the price of an SMS is € 0.1).
